Data Analyst, Wallingford, United Kingdom


Organization: CABI
Country: United Kingdom
Office: CABI Wallingford

What will you do ?


  • Collect and analyse large datasets using statistical and data visualization tools
  • Identify trends and patterns in data and develop insights to support and drive business decisions
  • Communicate findings to key stakeholders through presentations, dashboards and reports
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify areas for data-driven improvements, Proof of Concepts, and innovative uses of data
  • Continuously monitor data quality and ensure accuracy of data reporting to ensure trust and confidence.
  • Ensure the integrity of CABI’s Google Analytics data
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and new technologies to enhance data analysis capabilities so we are ready to field of data and visualization
  • Product ownership of the CABI Analytics Platform,an internal BI Reporting delivery system for, and take the service to the next level.

About You


  • You are a people person with an analytical mind and a passion for working with data from many sources.
  • You enjoy analysing and interpreting complex data sets, creating reports and visualizations to communicate findings and supporting decision-making.
  • You have a genuine interest in data visualization and innovation in this fast moving space and are driven to continually improve your knowledge.
  • You have a creative, problem solving outlook and the ability to explain complex data and analysis with staff at all levels.

What will be your salary ?


£27,000 -- £33,000 a year


What we can Offer you ?


  • Generous paid holiday entitlement and public holidays in line with local requirements
  • Defined contribution pension scheme with company contributions up to 10%
  • Group business travel cover
  • Flexible working arrangements
  • Family-friendly working policies
  • Part-funding for PhDs and other postgraduate courses
  • Training
  • Secondments
  • Recognition awards
