Research Analyst (Climate and Energy), Jakarta, Indonesia


Organization: Prospera
Country: Indonesia
Office: Prospera in Jakarta

What will you do ?

Prospera is a partnership facility between the Government of Australia (through Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade) and the Government of Indonesia. It aims to foster strong, sustainable, and inclusive economic growth in Indonesia. Commencing from 1 March 2018, Prospera is a planned five-year investment.


Responsibilities

  • Tertiary qualifications in economics and/or a related discipline are essential, with post-graduate qualifications preferred.
  • A quantitative modelling knowledge in this area is desirable. Computing proficiency with competency in the use of Microsoft products (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, etc.) as well as one or more data processing software such as STATA, R, EViews.
  • A minimum of 2 years’ professional experience working on green economic policy and related economic issues
  • Ability to conduct research and analysis on difficult, but well-defined tasks; articulate issues and recommend solutions.
  • Knowledge and experience working on the Indonesian economy and with Indonesian policymakers.
  • Excellent written communication skills with fluency in Bahasa Indonesia and English (both written and oral), including advocacy and presentation skills.

What will be your salary ?

$100,500 to $120,500 a year
