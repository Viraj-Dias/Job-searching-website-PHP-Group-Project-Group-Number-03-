Full Stack Web Developer, New Delhi, India


Organization:
Country: India
Office: IDinsight in New Delhi, India

What will you do ?


We are looking for a Full Stack Developer to produce scalable software solutions. You’ll be part of a cross-functional team that’s responsible for the full software development life cycle, from conception to deployment. As a Full Stack Developer, you should be comfortable around both front-end and back-end coding languages, development frameworks, and third-party libraries. We have a strong preference for a development stack that includes Flask, AWS, PostgreSQL, and a front end JavaScript framework like React or Angular. You should also be a team player with a knack for visual design and familiarity with Agile approaches to software development.

This role will be focused on effectively transitioning engineering practices at MSF-USA into an innovation-focused one though R&D and production-grade products. This transition will also require balancing new projects with existing maintained infrastructure. In both cases, having flexibility, assuming ownership and working iteratively on deliverables will be key to overall success.


Requirements


  • Proven experience as a Full Stack Developer or similar role. The experience required is 5 years.
  • Experience developing web applications for desktop and mobile.
  • Minimum of 2 years experience developing Flask applications.
  • Familiarity with hosting web applications on AWS (Elastic Beanstalk, RDS).
  • Knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Minimum 2 years experience with a front end JavaScript framework like React or Angular.
  • Familiarity with relational databases (PostgreSQL preferred), web servers (e.g. Apache) and UI/UX design
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills
  • Great attention to detail
  • Organizational skills
  • An analytical mind
  • Degree in Computer Science, or relevant field


Work authorization

All candidates not currently located in our country offices listed above will be expected to relocate to their office locations at the onset of their employment.
