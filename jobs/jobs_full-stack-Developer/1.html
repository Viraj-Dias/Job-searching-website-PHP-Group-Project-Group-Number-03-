Full stack developer/Cloud Engineer


Organization: BAO Systems
Country: Nigeria

What will you do ?


Make a positive impact on global public health while working in a fun, collaborative and flexible environment. We’re a nimble, close-knit group that values our teams opinion and contribution, allowing for everyone to make an impact to the work and company culture. We’re a rapidly growing multinational company. Our HQ is in Washington, D.C., with staff in the UK, Norway, Belgium, Portugal and Nigeria.


Responsibilities


  • Develops and deploy well architected solutions and infrastructure on the AWS platform using core AWS services. such as API Gateway, Lambda, Step Functions, SNS, DynamoDB, Glue, S3
  • Support front-end developers and data engineers in technical tasks
  • Develop APIs to support the UI and data exchange

Requirements

  • A bachelor’s degree in computer science, information technology, or related field and at least 5 years of experience developing web applications in AWS
  • Experience with Cloudformation, AWS Organizations, the Serverless Application Model (SAM), and AWS CLIs
  • Experience with core AWS services such as API Gateway, Lambda, Layers, Step Functions, SNS, DynamoDB, Glue, S3, and IAM
  • Experience monitoring and reporting on AWS solutions using Cloudwatch and related service integrations
  • Experience developing, documenting, and testing APIs that leverage third party identity providers for authentication and authorization
  • Strong Python programming skills with an understanding of virtual environments and version and dependency management
  • SQL and experience with relational and NoSQL databases
  • Prior experience working as part of an Agile development team

Desired Skills


  • React
  • Experience with data analytics projects
  • REDCap experience

Purpose of the role


We are looking for a motivated Full Stack Developer/Cloud Engineer to join our team. The ideal candidate is a domain expert who applies broad technical skills with deep industry knowledge and business acumen. Designs and implements cloud-native technical solutions to deliver data and analytics platforms to end users.
Work can be performed remote- East Coast time zone preferred


What will be your salary ?


$40,500 to $70,500 a year
