Thunderbird Full Stack Developer


Mozilla Organization:
Country: Canada

What will you do ?


We're looking for a Full-Stack Developer, who will play a pivotal role on a new product line. You will shape and build a new product from early prototyping, to MVPs, to finding product market fit. You are inventive, creative, and know just how far to plan ahead for a project that operates more like a startup than an established software product. You will work with a small cross-functional team that includes design and product management, and you will have wide latitude to operate independently with your teammates. You love to be invigorated by new challenges, you love working collaboratively, and you will have a team that supports you in making your very best contribution. We're committed to creating an amazing experience for our users, and you'll play a key part in this effort. You will be working with our existing staff and community members from all over the globe. We are key contributors to open source, allowing you to show your skills to the world both in terms of pure code and concrete product improvements. This is a remote , full time position. We expect excellent written communication skills so as to foster strong work coordination over email, video conferencing, Matrix, and Bugzilla.


  • Write code and tests, build prototypes, tackle problems with no clear solution, and collaborate with other designers and engineers as part of a global, distributed open source development team. This will involve technical decision making, code reviews (receiving and giving), engagement with contributors, planning and other collaborative development practices.
  • Help to build a comprehensive, threaded rework of the Thunderbird Calendar back-end from data storage to communication with calendar providers.
  • D Since we're a small team, you'll have a major impact on the design and development of Thunderbird code and take ownership of the projects you work on.ebug and fix problems to improve the quality of our complex code base, making a better experience for tens of millions of users.

Requirements


  • 3+ years of full-stack software development experience.
  • Proficiency with Javascript and/or Python, client- and server-side (Node.js, FastAPI).
  • Familiarity with a front-end frameworks including Svelte, Vue or React.
  • Experienced with SQL-based databases and NoSQL document stores.
  • Adept with Google Cloud Platform or Amazon Web Services.
  • Experience developing software products in a startup or highly lean environments.
  • Great communication skills (both written and verbal), documentation skills, and the ability to work with a multi-disciplinary, geographically distributed team.

We offer the following benefits


  • 24 paid time off days per year (prorated) + your birthday
  • Company/public holidays + year-end shutdown
  • Health, Dental, and Vision Insurance
  • Life Insurance + Accidental Death & Dismemberment
  • Short-Term Disability and Long-Term Disability
  • Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
  • Retirement Plan contributions
  • Remote Work Set-Up: latest hardware + internet reimbursement
  • Maternity and Parental leaves
