Senior Software Engineer: Flow, New York


Organization: Codeacademy
Country: United States of America
Office: Codecademy in New York,USA

What will you do ?


Work on a small, cross-functional team to create new projects that drive growth and build the foundation for meaningful changes to our global consumer business Partner with teams across the company to drive experimentation, growth, and A/B testing based on user research and data science insights Build products and experiences people love through intuitive design and clear user experiences


Responsibilities


  • Develop web applications with modern front-end frameworks and languages. We use React, Redux, Next.js, and Typescript

  • Build services using Ruby on Rails and/or Go

  • Design and iterate on experiments by utilizing A/B testing and working with counterparts in Data Science, Product, Design, and/or Business operations

  • Integrate with financial platforms that power our checkout experience such Recurly, Stripe, and more

  • Create user interfaces that are accessible for all by following WCAG, ARIA, and screen reader best practices

  • Put automated testing, tracking, and monitoring in place to ensure our website is always working as expected

  • Develop and continuously improve engineering standards within your team


Qualifications


  • Excitement about the opportunities and challenges of working on projects touching all areas of our codebase and associated microservices

  • Proven empathy for our users, such as a background in education or past experience using our site

  • You gained your software engineering skills without the benefit of a traditional CS education, whether you're self-taught, a bootcamp grad, or a Codecademy learner!

  • You love teaching people to code as a mentor, volunteer, writer, or teach


What will be your salary ?


$101,371 to $173,779 a year
