Frequently Asked Questions about CareerFinder


What is CareerFinder?

CareerFinder is a career website focused on the jobseeker’s journey. We share expert knowledge, tips, and tools to help everyone find their dream job.


What are CareerFinder's goals?

CareerFinder aims to make applying for a job fast and easy, for everyone. We believe that seasoned professionals and inexperienced candidates alike should be able to apply for any job they want, without being limited by their resume-making skills. We are committed to helping you every step along the way to your dream career.


Does CareerFinder have customer service?

Our customer service is available at all times, in all the languages we operate with. Want to download your account’s data or delete it entirely? Want to renew or cancel your subscription? You can easily do this anytime, we’re here for you! You can send us an email or a message using the contact form, or get in touch with our agents by phone during business hours.
